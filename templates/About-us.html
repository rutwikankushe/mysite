    THE Online CVTool STORY

    We discovered that students nowadays fabricate fraudulent credentials and submit them to their respective colleges. The problem discovered during the verification of a student's certificate was so severe that companies were forced to pay large sums of money in order to verify the legitimacy of the certificate submitted to them during interviews. The manual technique of certificate checking always has a time lag.

    Teachers and recruiters manually validate each student's or candidate's certificate, which takes a long time. This is what inspired us to create this online verification method.
