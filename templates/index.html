    WHAT IS ONLINE CERIFICATE VERIFICATION TOOL?

    We observed that nowadays students make fake certificates and submit it to their respective colleges.

    The problem found during verification of student’s certificate was so much that there were cases of employers spending lot of money in order to verify the authenticity of certificate presented to them during interviews.

    There is always a time delay in the manual method of certificate verification

    To provides easy access for organizations to check for the validity of the certificate of their employees remotely.

    To design a system that can help to minimize the high rate of manipulation and falsification of certificates in higher institutions.

    To resolve the issue of manual verification of certificates.

    To easily confirm the authenticity of any certificate by students

    HOW ONLINE CERTIFICATE VERIFICATION TOOL (CVT) WORKS??

    This is how the proposed system will work. Sequences of steps for implementation of the system are as follow:

    Provide a certificate with a certificate issuer link in it.

    Convert the image into text to get a required link to the certificate issuer.

    Comparing the obtained information with which is stored in the issuers database.

    Once the comparison is done, verify if the certificate is legit or forged.

    After successful verification, print the authenticate certificate
